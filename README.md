Right and left arrow keys allow the player to walk, and the space key is used for jumping.  
Your goal is to collect **30 coins** and reach the door to move to the next level.

There are enemies and spikes placed throughout the levels.  
If you collide with them, you will respawn at the beginning of the level and your **death counter increases by 1**.

You can see the coin amount, death amount, and whether the door is open through the **HUD** at the top of the screen.

The game contains **2 levels**.
